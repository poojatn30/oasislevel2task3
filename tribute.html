Puneeth Rajkumar

Puneeth Rajkumar (17 March 1975 - 29 October 2021), colloquially known as Appu, was an Indian actor, philanthropist, playback singer, television presenter, and producer, who worked in Kannada cinema. He was the youngest son of actor and matinee idol Dr. Rajkumar. He was one of the most popular actors in Kannada cinema.

On 29 October 2021, Puneeth complained of uneasiness to his wife Ashwini, and died on his way to a hospital in Bengaluru at the age of 46. The reason for his death was declared to be cardiac arrest.
